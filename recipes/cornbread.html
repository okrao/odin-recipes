Mom's Buttermilk Cornbread

Mom's Cornbread as shown on Allrecipes.com

Try some delicious homemade cornbread with this recipe. Either as a side to your soup beans or to enjoy on its own with butter, you too can make the most delicious cornbread from scratch in about 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups cornmeal
  • 1 cup of Buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, or more to taste
  • 2 Eight ounce canes of tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt, or more to taste
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder(if using self rising flour, this may not be needed)
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda (again, may not be needed if using self rising flour)

    • Directions

    1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
    2. Oil an 8-inch cast iron skillet. Heat in the preheated oven for 3 to 4 minutes.
    3. Whisk cornmeal, buttermilk, egg, oil, salt, baking powder, and baking soda together in a mixing bowl. Pour batter into the hot skillet.
    4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
    5. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 20 to 25 minutes. Remove from the oven and transfer cornbread to a serving plate.
